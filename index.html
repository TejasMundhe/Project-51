Origami Instructions and Diagrams


Step by step diagrams are the easiest way to demonstrate how to make beautiful origami objects. Finding reliable origami instructions on the internet can be tough and to help I've made this website by putting together overviews of some popular animal origami instructions paired with diagrams. On this website find the animal that you want to create and click on the corresponding picture to be sent to a page with the instructions on how to make that animal. Enjoy!
